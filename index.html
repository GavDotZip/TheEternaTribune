About Eterna City

Eterna City is a historic city located in the heart of the Sinnoh region. Founded over 1,000 years ago, the city is home to many ancient buildings and landmarks, including the Eterna Forest and the Eterna Gym.

Visitors to Eterna City can explore the city's rich history and culture, visit the Eterna Gym to battle against Gym Leader Gardenia, or venture into the Eterna Forest to catch Pokemon and uncover hidden treasures.
