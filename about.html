The Ancient Statue

The creation of DIALGA,
The giver of time...
In laughter, there is tears...
And, likewise it is with time.
The same time flows on.
For it is the blessing of DIALGA.

The birth of PALKIA.
The creator of parallel dimensions...
Alive, yet not alive...
Everything drifts in space...
To arrive in the same universe.
It is the blessing of PALKIA.


The Eterna City Space Statue is one of the City's most popular attractions. Thanks to a local man, as well as Pokemon Trainer Dawn, the inscriptions which were written in ancient Ransei text, have now been translated. The statue itself serves as a place of worship for the God of Time: Dialga, and the God of SPace: Palkia.
